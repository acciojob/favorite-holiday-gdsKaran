Switzerland


Switzerland is a beautiful country in Europe, known for its stunning Alps, charming villages, and peaceful lakes. It is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world.

  1. Mount Matterhorn – Iconic mountain peak
  2. Lake Geneva – Stunning lakeside beauty
  3. Jungfraujoch – The Top of Europe
  4. Zurich Old Town – Historical charm
  5. Rhine Falls – Europe’s largest waterfall
  • Fondue – Traditional Swiss cheese dish
  • Rösti – Crispy potato delicacy
  • Swiss Chocolate – World-famous delight
  • Raclette – Melted cheese specialty
  • Basler Läckerli – Traditional spiced biscuit
